Tteokbokki

Prep Time: 10 mins

Active Time: 10 mins

Total time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Onion sliced
  • 1/3 Cabbage sliced
  • 3 cups Korean Rice Cake
  • 2 cups Dashi Stock or Vegetable Stock
  • 1.5-2 tbsp of Gochujang
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 0.5 tbsp Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Garlic minced
  • 3 tsp Korean Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper optional
  • 1 cup Mozzarella Cheese shredded
  • 1 cup fish cakes optional

Instructions

  1. Turn the heat up to medium-high and add Gochujang, Sugar, Gochugaru, Honey, Garlic, black pepper, and Soy Sauce.
  2. Add a splash of dashi stock, Mix, and let it simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Pour in the rest of the dashi stock and bring to a simmer.
  4. Once simmering, add in onion and let it simmer for 3 minutes.
  5. Add rice cake and cook for another 5-6 minutes; make sure that all the rice cake is soaked in the sauce.
  6. Finally, add in any toppings of your choice, I like fish cake and cabbage, and simmer for another 3-4 minutes.
  7. Add 1 cup of cheese and broil it in the oven till cheese is melted, or cover with a lid on so the cheese fully melts.
