Tomato Curry Udon

Prep Time: 0 mins

Active Time: 15 mins

Total time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 curry cube
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 serving udon
  • Toppings: fish balls, mushroom, egg

Instructions

  1. Saute chopped tomatoes in a pot on medium-high for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add garlic and saute for 2-3 minutes, or until fragrant.
  3. Add soy sauce, ketchup, and water to the pot and let it simmer.
  4. Add in curry cubes, mix until curry cubes dissolve, then bring it to a simmer.
  5. Once curry cube has dissolved, add udon and toppings.
